Duck Game - Privacy

Personal Information

Duck Game save data is stored locally on your device and is never uploaded.

There are no user accounts to log in to and no interactions with other players.

Unity Analytics & Ads

Ads and analytics are services that I'm using that have been provided by Unity. I have informed Unity that there is the possibility that children under the age of 13 may choose to play this game. Unity's services adhere to the Children's Online Privacy and Protection Act (COPPA). The Unity Ads service will not select ads that are inappropriate for children under the age of 13 to view. Unity's privacy policy can be found here.

Some more information regarding Unity Analytics and the Children's Online Privacy and Protection Act can be found here
